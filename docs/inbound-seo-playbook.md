# Inbound & SEO playbook — get buyers to find Trishakti (2026-07-20)

Turn the website from a brochure into a lead machine. Six levers, in priority order.

---

## 1. SEO content — rank for what buyers actually Google
Buyers search problem/solution phrases. Target these (low-competition, high-intent for a Nepal factory):

| Keyword / phrase | Intent | Asset to build |
|---|---|---|
| **Nepal t-shirt manufacturer** | finding a factory | Home + /products/ optimised for this |
| **duty-free garment sourcing** | the tariff angle | Blog + the Duty-Savings Calculator page |
| **low MOQ clothing manufacturer** | small brands | Landing page "Low-MOQ knit manufacturing" |
| **Nepal vs Bangladesh sourcing** | comparison shoppers | Blog: honest comparison (you win on MOQ+ethics, not price) |
| **duty-free knitwear supplier EU / UK** | EU/UK buyers | Market landing pages (below) |
| **private label t-shirt manufacturer Nepal** | private-label brands | /products/ + blog |
| **DCTS duty-free clothing UK** | UK importers | UK landing page |
| **ethical clothing manufacturer Nepal** | values buyers | About/sustainability page |

**Content plan (1 post/week to start):**
1. "Duty-Free Sourcing from Nepal: The Complete 2026 Guide" (pillar page — links to everything)
2. "Nepal vs Bangladesh vs India: Where to Source Knitwear in 2026" (comparison — ranks + honest)
3. "How to Import Clothing from Nepal Duty-Free (EU / UK / Canada / Australia)"
4. "Low-MOQ Knitwear Manufacturing: A Guide for Growing Brands"
5. "Nepal's LDC Duty-Free Window Closes ~2029 — What Buyers Should Know"

**On-page basics for every page:** target keyword in the H1, title tag, first paragraph, and URL; add meta description; use real photos with alt-text; internal-link between pages.

## 2. The tools ARE SEO magnets + lead capture
Interactive tools attract links, shares, and long-tail searches on their own:
- **Duty-Savings Calculator** ✅ built (docs/duty-savings-calculator.html) → ranks for "garment import duty calculator", captures leads via WhatsApp
- **MOQ + ballpark price estimator** (tool #2) → "clothing manufacturing cost calculator"
- **Gated line-card PDF** (tool #3) → email capture
- **Get-a-Sample / RFQ form** (tool #4) → inbound inquiries
> ⚠️ Tools #3 & #4 need a **form backend** to capture emails / send to your inbox — a static site can't email by itself. Cheapest options: **Formspree**, **Netlify Forms**, **Google Forms**, or **Tally.so** (all free tiers). The calculator sidesteps this by routing to WhatsApp.

## 3. Google Business Profile — free map/search presence
You already have a Google Business contact. Set up the profile so you appear on Google Maps and local search:
- **business.google.com** → claim/create "Trishakti Apparel" → category **Clothing manufacturer** / **Garment exporter**
- Add: Gaidakot address, phone, website, hours, and **photos of the factory/products**
- Verify (postcard/phone). Then you show up when someone searches "garment manufacturer Nepal" on Maps.

## 4. Backlinks — raise domain authority (higher ranking)
Every reputable site linking to yours lifts your Google ranking. Get listed on:
- **GAN** (Garment Association Nepal) member directory → backlink
- **TEPC** exporter directory → backlink
- **Alibaba, Foursource** profiles → link to your site
- B2B directories: **Fibre2Fashion, Kompass, TradeIndia/ExportersIndia, Sourcify, Maker's Row**
- **Nepal Chamber of Commerce**, Narayangarh/Chitwan chamber
- Guest posts / mentions on sustainable-fashion blogs (your ethics story)
> Each is a free listing + a backlink. Do 1–2 per week.

## 5. Lead capture on every page — turn visitors into emails
Right now a visitor reads and leaves anonymous. Add:
- **Sticky "Get a quote" button** (→ WhatsApp or RFQ form) on every page
- **Gated line-card**: "Download our capability PDF" → email required → auto-email the PDF (needs Formspree/Mailchimp)
- **Exit intent / footer**: "Get our line-card + duty-free pricing guide" email box
- The **Duty-Savings Calculator** as a lead magnet on a dedicated page

## 6. Market landing pages — one per buyer geography (high-converting SEO)
Each ranks for its market's search and speaks directly to that buyer's duty rule:

| Page | Headline angle | Duty fact |
|---|---|---|
| **/uk-duty-free-sourcing** | "Duty-free knitwear for UK brands under DCTS" | 0% (vs 12% MFN); rule survives Nepal's LDC graduation |
| **/eu-duty-free-sourcing** | "0% duty into the EU under EBA" | 0% (vs 12%), through ~2029 |
| **/canada-duty-free-sourcing** | "18% saved vs MFN — duty-free to Canada (LDCT)" | 0% (vs **18%** — biggest saving) |
| **/australia-duty-free-sourcing** | "Duty-free knitwear for Australian brands" | 0% (vs 5%) |

Each page: the duty fact + savings calculator + line-card + "Get a quote" CTA.

## Priority order (what to do first)
1. **Put the Duty-Savings Calculator live** on the site (built ✅) — instant lead magnet
2. **Google Business Profile** — 30 min, free, immediate search presence
3. **Backlinks** — GAN, TEPC, Alibaba, Foursource, directories (1–2/week)
4. **Pillar blog post** ("Duty-Free Sourcing from Nepal 2026") + the 4 market landing pages

6. **Weekly content** to build ranking over 3–6 months

> SEO is a slow burn (3–6 months to rank), but it compounds — buyers find you 24/7 without outreach. Run it *alongside* the direct outreach, not instead of it.
