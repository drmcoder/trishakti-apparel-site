Why source from Nepal now

0% EU duty under Nepal's LDC / EBA status — vs. ~12% MFN. Because we cut & sew in Nepal, imported fabric still counts as Nepali origin (single-transformation rule).

Duty-free through ~2029 under the EU window — and even longer for the UK under DCTS, plus duty-free entry to Canada and Australia. Lock in your sourcing while the advantage lasts.
