# Site Improvements — learnings from 17 garment-exporter sites

Study (2026-07-13) of 10 global exporters (Shahi, Gokaldas, Pratibha, DBL, Crystal, TAL,
Appareify, Ninety Percent…) + 7 Nepal sites (Purnaa, Sherpa, garmentinnepal, Logo Industries,
GAN…). Full analysis in research transcript. Prioritized checklist below.

## The throughline
- **Global majors win on scale numbers + third-party proof** (Shahi: "100,000+ employees, 150M
  garments/yr"; Crystal: CDP A-List, Sustainalytics). We can't match volume — but we can
  approximate with an honest **stats strip**, a **transparency page**, and a **hydro-power +
  duty-free** angle.
- **Against Nepal peers we already lead** on design, schema, honesty. Fastest wins:
  **publish MOQ/lead-time (done ✓)** and **add client proof** — where even Purnaa is weak.
