# Export strategy — align the site to prior research (2026-07-11)

Source: the export-initiative research + decisions already made (see memory
`trishakti-export-project`). Full deep-research reports were session artifacts; this
captures the operative conclusions the site must reflect. Verify any figure before
publishing.

## The core edge (proven in research) — build the site around THIS
1. **Nepal LDC duty-free access** is the #1 hook. Under EU **EBA (Everything But Arms)**,
   Nepal garments enter the EU **duty-free**, and the **single-transformation rule of
   origin** means **Chinese fabric is fine** — the garment being *made* in Nepal confers
   origin. That's a real **~10–12% tariff saving** for an EU buyer vs. sourcing the same
   tee from a non-LDC country. → Put this front and center.
2. **Time pressure:** Nepal **LDC graduation is due Nov 2026** — after which EBA phases
   out (with a transition period). Frame as "lock in duty-free sourcing now."
3. Secondary edges: **own designs & spec charts**, **small/flexible MOQ**, **top quality**,
   **in-house Optitex CAD**.

## Market priority (reconcile with Santosh)
- Research concluded **EU-first** (because of EBA duty-free) + small private-label brands.
- Santosh's stated site pitch = **"general export / all markets."**
- **Resolution:** build an all-markets B2B site, but make the **EU duty-free advantage the
  hero hook** and add a dedicated "Duty-free EU sourcing" section. US/UK/AU buyers still
  benefit from LDC preferences too — mention broadly. Don't hide the EU angle; it's the
  strongest researched differentiator.

## ⚠️ Do NOT put on the site
- **Export cash subsidy / cash incentive.** Nepal's cash-incentive scheme (4–8%) had
  **new applications HALTED** by Ministry notice 2025-10-09; ~Rs 5bn unpaid. Do not
  promise or imply cash-incentive pricing to buyers.

## "Fish hooks" — where buyers meet factories (site must plug into these)
The site is one hook; it must reinforce and link to the others:
- **Google/SEO** — "Nepal t-shirt manufacturer", "duty-free knitwear Nepal", "LDC garment sourcing"
- **Alibaba / Foursource** RFQ profiles (consistent story + link)
- **Sedex / BSCI** databases (once audited)
- **GAN / TEPC** directories
- **Kathmandu buying houses**
- **LinkedIn / Instagram** (company pages)
- **Trade fairs**
→ Site should carry matching messaging + links so a buyer who finds any hook lands on a
consistent, credible site.
