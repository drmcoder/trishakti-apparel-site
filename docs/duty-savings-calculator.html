Nepal's cut-and-sew (single transformation) qualifies with imported fabric for the EU (EBA), UK (DCTS), Canada (LDCT — liberalised Jan 2025) and Japan (LDC single-stage rule). Australia requires ≥50% local content, so imported-fabric garments may not qualify. Nepal's EU/EBA duty-free runs through the ~2029 LDC-graduation transition. The USA grants Nepal no duty-free (NTPP expired). Rates are MFN on knitted apparel (HS 6109) and can change with trade policy.
