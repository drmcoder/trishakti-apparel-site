## Duty-free hook by market (lead with the right one)
- **EU:** EBA — duty-free, single transformation (Chinese fabric qualifies). LDC window to ~2029.
- **UK:** **DCTS** — duty-free incl. garments from imported fabric. ← strongest UK hook.
- **Canada:** LDCT — most fabric-friendly rule of all.
- **US:** no LDC duty-free — lead with quality / low MOQ / ethics / lowest-S.Asia tariff, not duty.
