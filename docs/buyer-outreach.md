## Duty-free hooks by market (use the right one per buyer)
- **EU:** EBA — duty-free, single transformation (Chinese fabric OK). LDC window → Nov 2026.
- **UK:** **DCTS** (Developing Countries Trading Scheme) — duty-free incl. garments from
  imported fabric. ← strongest hook for UK importers (Bradshaw Taylor).
- **US:** no LDC duty-free (GSP lapsed for apparel) — lead with quality/MOQ/ethics, not duty.
