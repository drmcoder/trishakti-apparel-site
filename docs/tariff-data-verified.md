## HONEST competitive read (no spin)
**Where Nepal WINS:**
- Duty-free EU/UK/Canada/Japan **vs China & India** (they pay 12–18%)
- Low/flexible **MOQ** (small factories) — mega-hubs won't take small runs
- **Ethical** story + full-package + own design

**Where Nepal does NOT win (be honest with buyers):**
- **vs Bangladesh:** Bangladesh also gets 0% into EU/UK/Canada, has **lower wages**, and **better (coastal) logistics**. Nepal does *not* beat Bangladesh on price or speed — only on MOQ flexibility + high-touch partnership.
- **Logistics:** landlocked (via Kolkata) = slower + costlier than coastal China/BD/India/Vietnam.
- **US:** Nepal ≈ India ≈ Bangladesh (~26.5%); only cheaper than China.
- **Scale/speed:** small industry, thin local fabric supply (imports from China = longer lead times).

**Bottom line for the pitch:** Nepal's real edge = **duty-free (vs China/India) + low MOQ + ethics + quality** for small/mid private-label buyers — NOT "cheapest" or "fastest."
